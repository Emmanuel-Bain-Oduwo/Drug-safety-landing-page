Safety standards

Simple checks that reduce preventable medicine harm.

Rx

Rational prescribing

Promote evidence-informed medicine choices, clear indications, dose clarity, and review dates before treatment starts.

ID

Patient verification

Make identity, allergies, pregnancy status, age, weight, diagnosis, and current medicines visible at each handover.

PK

Safe dispensing

Support label clarity, counselling, storage advice, interaction checks, and referral when a prescription needs clarification.

AE

Adverse event learning

Encourage timely reporting, respectful follow-up, and shared learning without blame when medicine-related harm is suspected.

Care workflow

One medication journey, shared by the whole team.

The experience is designed for high-volume clinical settings: quick to scan, clear under pressure, and useful from ward rounds to pharmacy counters.

  1. 01

    Assess

    Confirm diagnosis, risk factors, allergies, kidney or liver concerns, and treatment goals.

  2. 02

    Prescribe

    Choose the medicine, dose, route, frequency, duration, and monitoring plan with clear documentation.

  3. 03

    Dispense

    Check completeness, interactions, duplication, expiry, storage, and patient counselling needs.

  4. 04

    Administer

    Verify the patient and medicine at the point of care, then document administration and response.

  5. 05

    Monitor

    Track benefit, side effects, adherence barriers, lab needs, and suspected adverse drug reactions.

Built for real care teams

Different roles, one shared safety language.

Doctors

Concise medicine review prompts, safer dose decisions, treatment duration reminders, and escalation cues.

Pharmacists

Prescription screening, counselling priorities, interaction checks, stock substitution notes, and referral flags.

Nurses

Administration checks, missed-dose visibility, patient observation notes, and rapid reporting of safety concerns.

Patients and families

Plain-language medicine instructions, side-effect awareness, adherence support, and questions to ask before leaving care.
